Introducing Piksi

Piksi is designed for autonomous vehicle and device guidance, aerial surveying and other use cases requiring high precision GPS/GNSS. It delivers RTK functionality for centimeter-level relative positioning accuracy in a 53x53mm form factor. The Piksi receiver supports high-performance, onboard Digital Signal Processor (DSP) and a flexible correlation accelerator.

Features

  • Centimeter-accurate relative positioning (Carrier phase RTK)

  • 10 Hz position/velocity/time solutions for fast updates

  • Open-source software and board design

  • Low power consumption: 500mW typical

  • Small form factor:
    53x53mm

  • USB and dual UART connectivity

  • Full-rate raw sample pass-through over USB

  • 3-bit, 16.368 MS/s L1 front-end supports GPS, GLONASS, Galileo and SBAS signals
