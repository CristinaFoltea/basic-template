Agriculture

Agriculture is taking the next leap forward with the ability to automate vehicles and implements. Whether built into navigation systems or added as after-market capabilities, Swift’s RTK solutions can help improve fuel usage, monitor fertilizer and seed placement, track equipment and aid navigation-focused tasks.

  • High-Volume Affordability

    At a fraction of the cost of hardware- based, traditional GPS solutions, Swift solutions make it possible to achieve new functionality while maintaining profitability.

  • Minimizing Risk

    Minimize the risk of solution failure with Swift. We design for a broad range of field conditions to help ensure project success. A low cost with the ability to upgrade software later delivers further assurance and future proofing.

  • Small, Efficient Footprint

    Our sleek 53x53mm size makes it easier to fit sophisticated navigation capabilities into smaller form factors. Get high-precision accuracy and future-proofed flexibility in a compact footprint.
