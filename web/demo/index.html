Introducing Piksi

Piksi™ is designed for autonomous vehicle and device guidance, aerial surveying, and other use cases requiring high-precision GPS/GNSS. It delivers RTK functionality for centimeter-level relative positioning accuracy in a low power, 53x53mm form factor.

  • Centimeter-accurate relative positioning (Carrier phase RTK)

  • Low power consumption: 500mW typical

  • Open-source software and board design

  • Small form factor:
    53x53mm
