Survey

Survey and control applications span many industries and environments. From precision surveys to numerous precision control use cases, customers around the world use Swift RTK solutions for clear-sky:

  • Building, construction and real estate surveys
  • Industrial inspection
  • Agricultural surveys
  • Boundary surveys
  • Soil and topographic surveys
  • Earth movement detection
  • Landfill mapping
  • Search and rescue
  • Wind turbine measurement
  • Security
  • Special-effects camera tracking for media and sports
  • Follow-me drones for media and sports videos

  • Improve Precision and Longevity

    Swift RTK solutions integrate easily with UAVs to achieve precision landing scenarios. For example, UAVs using Swift RTK can initiate a landing at 30’ instead of 300’. The ability to control landings more precisely results in a longer UAV service life.

  • Improve Output Quality

    High-precision RTK technology increases the quality of final orthomosaics to improve decision-making.

  • Unprecedented Affordability

    At a fraction of the cost of hardware-based, traditional GPS solutions, Swift solutions make it possible to achieve new functionality cost-effectively for large deployments.

  • Reduce Pilot Training Costs

    High-precision accuracy and easy controls let even novice pilots control UAVs more accurately to reduce training costs.

  • Reduce UAV Lifecycle Costs

    With Swift solutions, you can use existing base stations for ground control. Swift RTK technology is software-based, which eliminates the need for costly, inflexible hardware systems and enables you to reduce capital cost for improved profitability. In addition, you can use Swift RTK technology with existing base stations to avoid having to purchase new ground systems.
